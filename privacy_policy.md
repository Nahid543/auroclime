# Privacy Policy for Auroclime

**Last Updated:** December 12, 2024

## Introduction

Auroclime ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our weather application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **GPS Coordinates**: We collect your device's precise location (latitude and longitude) to provide accurate local weather forecasts.
- **Location Name**: We use reverse geocoding to display your city/region name.

### Stored Preferences
- Temperature unit preference (Celsius/Fahrenheit)
- Wind speed unit preference
- Auto-refresh interval settings
- Saved locations (if you manually add them)
- Notification settings and preferences
- Onboarding completion status

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Fetch and display weather data for your location
- Show UV index, air quality, and other weather metrics
- Send weather notifications (only if you enable them)
- Remember your app preferences
- Cache weather data for offline viewing

## Data Storage

- **Local Only**: All data is stored locally on your device
- **No Cloud Sync**: We do not upload or sync your data to our servers
- **No Account Required**: You don't need to create an account to use Auroclime

##Third-Party Services

### Open-Meteo API
We use Open-Meteo (https://open-meteo.com) to fetch weather data. When you use our app:
- Your GPS coordinates are sent to Open-Meteo's API to retrieve weather information
- Open-Meteo has its own privacy policy: https://open-meteo.com/en/terms

### Google Play Services
- We use Google Play Services for location services
- Subject to Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Sharing

We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with advertisers
- Track your location for any purpose other than weather
- Collect analytics or usage data
- Require registration or login

## Permissions Explained

### Location Permission (Required)
- **Why**: To get weather for your current location
- **When**: Only when you open the app or refresh weather
- **How**: We send coordinates to Open-Meteo API to fetch weather data

### Notification Permission (Optional)
- **Why**: To send weather alerts  and daily summaries
- **When**: Only if you enable notifications in settings
- **Control**: You can disable anytime in app settings

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Deny location permission (app will prompt to add location manually)
- Clear cached data anytime (Settings → Data Management → Clear Cache)
- Disable notifications (Settings → Notifications)
- Uninstall the app (removes all data from your device)

## Children's Privacy

Auroclime does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Our app does not require any personal information beyond location for functionality.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Data Retention

- Location data: Not stored permanently, only cached temporarily
- Preferences: Stored until you clear app data or uninstall
- Weather cache: Automatically refreshed based on your settings

## Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:
- All data stored locally on your device
- No transmission of personal data except GPS coordinates to weather API
- Standard Android security practices
